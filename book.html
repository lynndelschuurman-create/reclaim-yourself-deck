Reclaiming My Place in My Own Life

A poetic journey home to myself

Reclaiming My Place in My Own Life is not just a collection of poems - These words are a reclamation - of voice, worth, and your power within.

A poetry collection born from the ache of becoming, a journey of remembering, healing, and returning home to myself. Each piece is a reclamation of my voice, worth, softness and belonging. Written in the moments I forgot my own power and in the remembering that followed.
